Status of the Fishery (as of 07/18/1991)

NOP WAS ABUNDANT (21.5/GILLNET). AVERAGE SIZE WAS SMALL (1.32 LB) AND MEDIAN LENGTH WAS 18 12-25 WITH 60% OF MEASURED FISH COMING FROM THE 1988 YEAR CLASS. GROWTH WAS GOOD. BLG (78.5/TRAPNET) WAS ALSO ABUNDANT WITH SMALL (0.08 LB) AVERAGE SIZE. MEDIAN LENGTH WAS 4.0 AGE III-VIII WERE PRESENT WITH AGES III-V BEING MOST COMMON IN THE SAMPLE. GROWTH WAS SLOW AS WELL. BLC (1.0/TRAPNET) WERE CAUGHT IN LOW NUMBERS FOR THIS LAKE CLASS BUT SIZE (0.28 LB) WAS AVERAGE. THREE SLOW-GROWING AGE CLASSES WERE PRESENT IN THE COMBINED CATCH FROM GILLNETS AND TRAPNETS. LMB WAS SAMPLED ONLY IN GILLNETS (ONE FISH). YEB (13.5/TRAPNET) WAS PRESENT IN AVERAGE NUMBERS AND SIZE (0.39 LB). THE MEDIAN LENGTH WAS 8.5 POPULATION WAS SIMILAR IN COMPOSITION TO THAT SEEN IN THE 1973 INITIAL LAKE SURVEY. THE POPULATION DID NOT SEEN UNCHARACTERISTIC FOR A SMALL, SOFT- WATER LAKE WITH LOW FERTILITY.
